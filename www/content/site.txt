Title: Judith Achumba-Wöllenstein

----

Headline: My name is Judith Achumba-Wöllenstein — interior designer, art director, editorial writer and psychology of design specialist.

----

Text:

I provide psychology-based interior design services via my studio’s website: (link: https://atelier.akuko.studio text: Atelier Akuko).

I'm also the creative director of the Fashion Psychology Magazine (link: https://hajinsky.com text: HAJINSKY), which provides research insights into the human experience with fashion.

Say hi, to discuss ways we can work together.
